OC Strikers FC

OC Strikers FC, based in the vibrant heart of Orange County, is a soccer team infused with local spirit and an unyielding drive for success. This ambitious club began with a simple yet profound mission: to elevate local talent to the national stage, celebrating the love of soccer shared by its community. The dedication and hard work of everyone involved have seen this dream rapidly start to become a reality, as the team makes its exciting ascent into the United Soccer Premier League (UPSL), the fourth division of semi-professional soccer in the United States.

From humble beginnings, the OC Strikers FC has grown, both in size and in skill, through nurturing a culture of resilience, teamwork, and continuous improvement. Their journey to the UPSL has been nothing short of inspirational, filled with challenging matches and memorable victories that have bonded the team and fans alike. Coaches with deep experience and a genuine passion for development have been pivotal, turning raw talent into skilled players who excel under pressure.

The heart of OC Strikers FC lies in its community support. Every game is a gathering of enthusiastic fans who bring an electric atmosphere to the stadium, cheering passionately, rain or shine. This support has been a cornerstone of the team’s success, providing the players with an extra burst of energy and determination on the field. As they gear up for their debut in the UPSL, OC Strikers FC is not just aiming for wins, but to inspire and unite their community through the beautiful game of soccer. Each match is more than a game; it's a celebration of local talent, community spirit, and the universal language of soccer that brings people together.

About Us

OC Strikers FC: Champions with Local Talent

OC Strikers FC embodies the spirit of community and the thrill of soccer in Southern California. Based in the vibrant regions of Orange County and Los Angeles County, our team prides itself on showcasing the talents of homegrown players. Each member of our squad not only represents the best of local athleticism but also carries the essence of their hometowns onto the pitch, making us true local heroes in the realm of semi-professional soccer.

Meet the Team

Micah Vieira - Forward

A standout player from Long Beach, Micah is known for his explosive speed and precise finishing. His charismatic presence on the field exemplifies the energetic spirit of LA.

Alex Martinez - Winger

Alex's roots in Westmister shine through his agile gameplay and smart decision-making. He orchestrates our midfield with finesse, displaying the quintessential Orange County resilience.

Michael Martinez - Defender

From Garden Grove, Michael is the backbone of our defense. His strategic positioning and calm demeanor under pressure secure our backline, reflecting the disciplined nature of his upbringing.

Yussif Dakelbab - Defender

Hailing from Huntington Beach, Yussif’s robust playing style and tactical intelligence make him a key asset in both offensive and defensive scenarios.

Ahmed Karim - Forward

A fierce competitor from Irvine, Ahmed brings versatility and sharp instincts to the forward line, often outsmarting defenders with his quick moves.

Anaas Karim - Defender

Brother to Ahmed and also from Irvine, Anas is known for his strong defensive skills and the ability to read the game exceptionally well, providing stability and strength to our defense.

Aldo Palacios - Defender

Aldo, from Huntington Beach, combines strength and strategy to fortify our defense, embodying the robust character of his city.

Gabe Nunez - Winger

From the bustling streets of Fountain Valley, Gabe’s agility and flair on the wings captivate fans, bringing a burst of creativity and speed to our play.

Erik - GoalKeeper

As our primary goalkeeper from Garden Grove, Erik's leadership and decisive saves are vital to our team’s success, reflecting his deep commitment and passion for soccer.

Yazan Khawaldeh - MidFielder

Coming from Tustin, Yazan's robust playing style and rapid pace make him a key asset in both offensive and defensive scenarios.
